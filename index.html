Frequently Asked Questions

Is Polban free to use?

Polban offers a free tier with all the essential features for managing your finances. We also have a premium subscription with advanced features like unlimited accounts, detailed analytics, and priority support.

How does Polban keep my financial data secure?

We take security very seriously. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use Supabase as our backend provider, which offers robust, enterprise-grade security to ensure your data is always safe.

Can I import my transaction data from another app or my bank?

Yes, our premium version allows you to import transactions from a CSV file, making it easy to switch to Polban from another app or to import your bank statements.

What languages does Polban support?

Polban is designed to be multilingual. We currently offer full support for English, Persian (Farsi), Turkish, and Arabic, including proper right-to-left (RTL) layout and typography. We are planning to add more languages in the future.

How does the multi-currency feature work?

Polban allows you to track transactions in multiple currencies. You can set a base currency, and all transactions will be automatically converted to this currency for unified reporting and analysis. Exchange rates are updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Can I schedule recurring transactions?

Yes, Polban has a powerful scheduled transactions feature that allows you to set up recurring payments and incomes. You can customize frequency, start/end dates, and even set up complex patterns for irregular payments.

What types of reports can I generate?

Polban offers comprehensive reporting capabilities including financial charts, pie charts, balance sheets, and detailed transaction reports. You can export these reports in PDF, Excel, or CSV formats for sharing or further analysis.

Why Polban?

Polban simplifies personal accounting with support for Persian, English, Turkish, and Arabic, multi-currency management, Persian and Gregorian calendars, and advanced financial charts. Incomes are always positive, expenses negative, and reports are unified in your base currency (USD). Automate recurring payments with scheduled transactions and speed up entry with smart suggestions. Use transaction filters for quick searches and balance sheets for liquidity analysis. Share reports easily with PDF exports and saved charts. Polban is your go-to app for smart asset and transaction management!
